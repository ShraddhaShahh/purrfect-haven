How it all started!

Purrfect Haven is more than an organization; it's a heartfelt journey that began with a simple act of compassion and grew into a mission of love and care. The story behind our creation is one that reminds us of the incredible impact that a single act of kindness can have on both human and feline lives. Founded by a brother and sister duo, Shraddha and Vivek, Purrfect Haven's genesis was driven by a shared admiration for the feline world. Their beloved companions, Mojo and Jojo, transformed from vulnerable souls to confident family members, igniting a passion to create a haven for cats in need. Purrfect Haven stands today as a testament to their dedication, rescue efforts, and unwavering compassion. Each adoption, each purr, and each loving gaze reaffirms the significance of their mission. Driven by unwavering compassion, Purrfect Haven invites you to join their story of transforming lives, ignited by a simple act of kindness.
